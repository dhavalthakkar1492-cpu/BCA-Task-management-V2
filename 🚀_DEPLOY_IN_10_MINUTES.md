## ⚡ **THE FASTEST PATH (10 min)**

### **Step 1:** Create Free GitHub Account
Go to: https://github.com/signup  
Time: 2 minutes

### **Step 2:** Create Repository
Go to: https://github.com/new  
Name: `ca-firm-portal`  
Time: 1 minute

### **Step 3:** Upload 5 Files to GitHub
1. Click "Add file" → "Upload files"
2. Drag & drop these 5 files:
   - App.jsx
   - package.json
   - tailwind.config.js
   - postcss.config.js
   - README.md
3. Click "Commit changes"  
Time: 1 minute

### **Step 4:** Deploy to Vercel (Free!)
1. Go to: https://vercel.com
2. Click "Sign Up" → "Continue with GitHub"
3. Click "Import Project"
4. Select "ca-firm-portal"
5. Click "Import"
6. Wait 30 seconds...  
Time: 1 minute

### **Step 5:** Get Your Live URL
✅ Portal is LIVE!  
You'll get a URL like: `https://ca-firm-portal-abc123.vercel.app`

Time: 1 minute

### **Step 6:** Share with Team
Send URL to your team - they can access immediately!  
Time: 1 minute

### **Step 7:** Test Features (Optional)
- Click Dashboard → See KPI cards
- Click Tasks → See task table
- Click Invoices → See invoices
- Click Staff → See staff cards  
Time: 2 minutes
